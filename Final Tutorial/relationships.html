Relationships

How does Relationships work?

FAQ

Can anyone support each other? No.
Is there a limit to how many supports I can have? No.
Any prerequisites for a relationship? Just increase your supports rank!
To build a support (relationship) you must fight alongside your intended partner or heal them. Each of your allies grants you a variety of stat boosts. As your relationship grows the boost they give increase.

How does it benefit me?

By have characters get married, they have children that in turn join your army. The classes they inherit depend entirely upon their mother in this installment. The mother also modifies their max stats.
In the image above Kana is talking to her mother, Sakura. A child like Kana can only support other children or her parents. This applies to every child, but they can support your avatar.
