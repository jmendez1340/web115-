My Castle

How does Relationships work?

What can you do?

Interact with others
Obtain Items
Partake in events
Bond with your allies
Customize a character's appearance
Visit other and Battle
Never before has the Fire Emblem series done something quite like this! You can visit and battle a friend or gather items for your journey.

How does it benefit me?

You can obtain rare minerals and different type of food which can be used to buy accessories for your units or feed your own resident dragon. Talking to the castle residents can increase relationships.
To build your castle you must complete chapters or visits another castle via wireless connection or wifi. You can create statues of your characters, armories, arenas, hot springs and many more.
