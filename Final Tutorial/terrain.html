How does Terrain work?

Terrain can give defensive boosts, increase evasion and heal your units. At the same time there are some terrain that can lower your defense and evasion and drain your units Hp.
In the image above the mountain area gives units who aren't fliers +2 to their defences and adds 20 to their evasion rate. This is especially useful for fragile units or dodge masters.
