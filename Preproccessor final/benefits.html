Benefits

Who benefits?

    Every person who values cleanliness.

  • You
  • Your family
  • Future generations
  • Animals
  • Our Planet

How do I personally benefit?

    Benefits include:

  • Cleaner Society
  • Nicer smell.
  • Cans can be recycled for money.
  • You can now use beaches without fear of pollution.

When will you benefit?

It depends entirely on what you contribute. If you recycle cans you can make money quickly. Others will help not only us, but our future as well and you can't put a price on that.
